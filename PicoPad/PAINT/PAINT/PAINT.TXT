^WRIGHT^0 ............... Right
^WUP^0 .................. Up

^WB + Right or Left^0 ... Background Color
^WX + Up or Down^0 ...... Brush radius size
^WX + Right or Left^0 ... Step length
^WY^0 ................... quit to menu
